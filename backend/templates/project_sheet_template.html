The new four-story PS 163 Arthur A. Schomburg Elementary School replaces the existing three-story building built in the 1970s and renovated in the 1990s. This new school will be an L-shaped, energy efficient, modern 82,000 SF steel structure building with a composite deck. Its complex site location at a heavily sloped corner site required substantial rock excavation to build underground structures. An open stair connects the ground floor with the lower level that houses public artwork and serves as main access to the lower outdoor playground.
